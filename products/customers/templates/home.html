An Online Marketplace for Agricultural ProductsProject Description:AgriMart is a web-based platform designed to empower farmers by providing easy access to a wide range of agricultural products. The application allows farmers to browse, select, and purchase fertilizers, seeds, tools, and advanced agricultural products from the comfort of their homes. Leveraging modern e-commerce technologies, AgriMart ensures that farmers can have these products delivered directly to their doorstep, much like popular online retail platforms such as Flipkart. The platform aims to streamline the supply chain for agricultural products, making high-quality resources more accessible and affordable to farmers, regardless of their location. By integrating features like detailed product descriptions, user reviews, and secure payment gateways, AgriMart creates a reliable and user-friendly shopping experience tailored specifically for the agricultural community
